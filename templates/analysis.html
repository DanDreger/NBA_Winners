Does spending money on offense translate to wins in the playoffs?

Lets take a look:

One of the most surprising findings of this study is the inefficicency in defense spending come playoff time. At best there is no correlation between defensive spending and playoff wins. And it's possible that spending more money on defense may hurt a teams chances of winning addiional playoff games. Teams that invest only 20-25% of their budgets fair just as well in the playoffs as teams spending much more!

Positional Spending

Does spending by position affect a teams ability to win or lose?

Lets take a look:

There can be no definitive answer on "how to spend money" on a positional level with out further research. But what these charts do tell us is how teams are spending their money.

Its clear from the chart that teams are devaluing running backs, with most of those players earning the least % of the cap. Then, on a per/player basis, Receivers earn more than running backs but less than Offensive Linemen. And Quarterbacks, not surprisingly, make the most.
